Welcome!

DCAA-compliant time management system homepage

DCAA stands for "Defense Contract Audit Agency". The DCAA is the government agency responsible for auditing contracts from the Department of Defense. This web-app allows for you to stay in compliance with all government laws and being prepared for possible audits.

A brief explanation for how each user should use this application:

Employers:

Your job is to invite your employees to this application, create projects, and assign them to the projects. Over the course of time, you will evaluate and audit all time logs, expense logs, and expense requests by your employees.

Employees:

you will create time logs and expense logs pertaining to all actions done under specified assigned projects.

Auditor:

You will have an employer log in and navigate to the application audit log and evaluate the data presented to make sure it's under audit protocol and following DCAA compliance
